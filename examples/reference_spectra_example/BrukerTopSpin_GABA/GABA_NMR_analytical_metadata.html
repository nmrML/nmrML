Data Processing

1D proton spectra and carbon are phased manually. The baselines are corrected automatically. The spectra were referenced to the TSP peak of which is set to zero automatically and manually for the proton on carbon.

 

Peak Picking

The PP is initially performed automatically in the proton spectrum (expno 4) and on the carbon spectrum (expno 8). The list of peaks is obtained verified. If necessary, peaks which are not associated with the studied molecule (dirt, noise, etc ...) are removed manually. The data is exported directly from TOPSPIN3.2 to an EXCEL file.
